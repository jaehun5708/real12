Air Quality Dashboard Information

Purpose

This dashboard is designed to help users monitor real-time air quality conditions and gain a clear understanding of air quality indicators. By visually presenting data, it makes it easier to comprehend information related to air pollution.

Key Air Quality Indicators

PM2.5: Fine particulate matter that can have severe health impacts due to its small size.

PM10: Particulate matter associated with respiratory diseases.

NO2 (Nitrogen Dioxide): A major pollutant generated from traffic and industrial activities.

CO (Carbon Monoxide): A dangerous gas that can cause oxygen deficiency.

SO2 (Sulfur Dioxide): A key pollutant resulting from the combustion of fossil fuels.
